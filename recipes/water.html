The Water Recipe


Water may not seem like an ingredient that needs prep work.
However, in the context of growing there are necessary steps to be taken.

Ingredients

  • One gallon of Water (preferably filtered water)
  • Water nutrients (Ocean solution)

Directions


In order to avoid over-feeding the plants with this solution,
we will create a 1% diluted mix.
  1. Pour out 1L of water
  2. Measure out 0.25 oz of nutrient solution*
  3. Add nutrient solution to the water
  4. Mix thoroughly

*Use directions on the packaging of chosen solution to decide how diluted the mixture needs to be.
